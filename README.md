# Marketing A/B Test Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the results of a marketing A/B testing campaign to evaluate the effectiveness of different marketing strategies and determine whether the new campaign significantly improved customer conversion.

The analysis combines exploratory data analysis, statistical reasoning, and visualization to provide data-driven recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Understand customer behavior across test groups
- Compare the performance of Campaign A and Campaign B
- Analyze conversion rates
- Identify statistically meaningful differences
- Generate actionable marketing insights

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset includes customer-level marketing information such as:

- Campaign Group (Control / Test)
- Conversion Status
- Customer Attributes
- Marketing Exposure
- Engagement Metrics

## 🔍 Business Questions

- Which campaign generated a higher conversion rate?
- Does the new campaign outperform the existing strategy?
- Are customer segments responding differently?
- What insights can improve future marketing campaigns?
